NSF Student Travel Grant

 

ISVLSI 2020 invites applications for several NSF student travels grants from the students of the USA Universities. Each student travel awards will have $500 to partially cover the cost of travel including flight, hotel, etc. In addition, the registration to ISVLSI 2020 is complimentary for the awardees*.

The students will need to include the following as the application package (as one PDF file):

  1. Application Form with Questionnaires and Information.
  2. A 2-page Resume.
  3. A letter from a Faculty Mentor or University Chair confirming to cover any additional funds.
  4. A letter of confirmation from the student stating that he/she will attend the conference if travel award is provided.

Application need to be filled/submitted online using EDAS.

The deadline of application submission: April 7th, 2020

The NSF travel grants are open to both graduate and undergraduate students from the USA Universities. A certain number of NSF travel grants are allocated for underrepresented students. A certain number of NSF travel grants are allocated for students who will attend the conference for the first time and do not have a paper/presentation/poster at the conference.

For additional questions on the NSF student travel grant, contact Steering Committee Vice-Chair at: saraju.mohanty@unt.edu
