Call for Industry Forum Papers/Talks



Industry Forum Sessions

The ISVLSI 2024 is inviting submissions for an industry forum session, where experts, researchers, and stakeholders working on VLSI and emerging technologies are encouraged to present on emerging problems, or recent technical trends in computer architecture, IoT systems, security, artificial intelligence, machine learning, edge intelligence, data analytics, autonomous systems, or any other relevant topic that are would be of interest to ISVLSI participants.

  • Each speaker at industry forum session has to submit a 1-2 pages extended abstract for inclusion in the conference proceedings.
  • Accepted extended abstracts will be included in the conference proceedings. The ISVLSI proceedings will be indexed in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.
  • All extended abstracts will be reviewed before being published in the official proceedings of the conference in a separate track.
  • The online submission system EasyChair will be used for review of the extended abstracts.

Submission Guidelines: Prepare a single PDF file (not exceeding 2 pages) using the IEEE conference template, which can be found here: (Link to Template).

Submit your Industry Forum paper in EasyChair.
