NSF Student Travel Grant

IEEE-ISVLSI 2024 (www.ieee-isvlsi.org) invites applications for several NSF student travels grants for the students from US Universities. Each student travel award will have $1,000 to partially cover the cost of travel including flight, hotel, registration, etc.

The application needs to be filled/submitted online.
NSF Travel Grant Application (Google Form)

Students must provide the following items in the application:

  • Application Form with Questionnaires and Information.
  • A 2-page Resume.
  • A letter from a Faculty Mentor or University Chair confirming to cover any additional funds.
  • A letter of confirmation from the student stating that he/she will attend the conference if the travel award is provided.

These can all be submitted via the Application form online, by clicking above or visiting here.

The Deadline for Application Submission is June 30, 2024.


The NSF travel grants are open to both graduate and undergraduate students from US Universities. A certain number of NSF travel grants are allocated for underrepresented students. A certain number of NSF travel grants are earmarked for students who will attend the conference for the first time and do not have a paper/presentation/poster at the conference.

For additional questions on the NSF student travel grants, contact Steering Committee Vice-Chair at saraju.mohanty@unt.edu.
