Important Author Information

At least one author per paper must be registered as an AUTHOR at the full Member / Non-Member Registration Rate, regardless of whether or not they are students.

  • Each Author Registration is valid for ONE paper.
  • If necessary, up to 4 additional papers can be purchased per author registration for an additional fee of US$590 per paper. For these additional papers, if any of the co-author other than the primary registrant will be presenting the paper, please fill their name during registration and these co-authors will be included in the conference registrant list.
  • You may use Easychair to locate your Paper ID.

See the Cvent Registration System for more information.

For questions regarding paper submissions, please contact Himanshu Thapliyal at ISVLSI2024@gmail.com.

Cancellation Policy

All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to registration+ISVLSI@computer.org by 14 June 2024, 11:59 PM Eastern Time. There will be an administrative fee of 50% of the registration fee for canceled registrations.

Author registrations are non-refundable.
