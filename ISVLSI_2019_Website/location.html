ISVLSI’19 – Social Events

 

The Conference organizers have planned exciting event on Dinner Cruise for the ISVLSI'19 attendees that is full of fun, music, delicious food!

Event time: July 16th, 2019 at 6:30 PM

Bus pick up:

There will be two coach buses on July 16th, 2019 at 4:45 PM, in front of the Academic I building for transportation to the Sun Dream Yacht Charters. The bus will stay on site and at 11:30 PM, the guests will re-board to be transported back to FIU-BBC, front of the Academic I building. The bus will be making a couple of stops while enroute to the BBC, to nearby hotels.

Location:

2950 NE 32nd Ave, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33308

m/y Catalina DINNER Cruise

Sun Dream Yacht Charters is Florida's premiere dinner cruise and party boat company. They serve gourmet food that is prepared onboard for you and your guests as you cruise along the Intracoastal waterway. Join Sun Dream Yacht Charters for our social event to enjoy twinkling skylines and silhouettes of dancing palms set against the unforgettable fiery sunsets of South Florida.
