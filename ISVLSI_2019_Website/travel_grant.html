NSF Student Travel Grant

 

ISVLSI 2019 invites applications for several NSF student travels grants from the students of the USA Universities. Each student travel awards will have $500 to partially cover the cost of travel including flight, hotel, etc. In addition, the registration to ISVLSI 2019 is complimentary for the awardees.

The students will need to include the following as the application package:

  1. Application Form with Questionnaires and Information.
  2. A 2-page Resume.
  3. A letter from a Faculty Mentor or University Chair confirming to cover any additional funds.
  4. A letter of confirmation from the student stating that he/she will attend the conference if travel award is provided.

Application need to be filled/submitted online following the link: Application form link.

The deadline of application submission: June 7, 2019

The NSF travel grants are open to both graduate and undergraduate students. A certain number of NSF travel grants are allocated for underrepresented students. A certain number of NSF travel grants are allocated for students who will attend the conference for the first time and do not have a paper/presentation/poster at the conference.

For additional questions on the NSF student travel grant, contact a General Chair at: saraju.mohanty@unt.edu
