ISVLSI 2011 PhD Forum


The PhD forum is hosted by the ISVLSI 2011 conference which is held in Chennai, India, from July 4-6, 2011. This Forum is a great opportunity for PhD students to present their thesis work to a broad audience in the VLSI community from both industry and academia. The PhD Forum may also help students to establish contacts for entering the job market. On the other side, representatives from industry and academia get a glance of state-of-the-art in system design and design automation.

Submission Website

Who can present their work at the PhD forum?

Students who have finished their PhD thesis within the last 12 months or students who are close to finish to their thesis work

From all submitted PhD poster paper, the number of 10 papers will be selected to be presented at the ISVLSI 2011 conference. Furthermore, the selected poster papers will be published (2 pages) in the Conference Proceedings.

The selected PhD Students who represent the submitted topic have the benefits:

  • of a poster presentation in A0 format at the ISVLSI 2011 PhD Forum
  • to have contacts to professionals from industry and academia
  • to have the possibility to distribute flyers summarizing their PhD work
  • The chance to receive the best PhD poster award of the ISVLSI 2011 (for this purpose the program committee of the ISVLSI 2011 PhD Forum evaluates the review results and the quality of the poster / the presentation respectively at the conference). The best PhD poster award will be granted at the conference dinner.

How to submit:

  • Please submit a 2-page extended abstract describing the novelties and advantages of the thesis. Please use the IEEE 2 column template which can be found with this weblink:
     http://www.ieee.org/web/publications/pubservices/confpub/AuthorTools/conferenceTemplates.html
  • Please don’t exceed the page number limitation and use only the template from IEEE
  • The submission interface is available at:

http://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=isvlsi2011phdforum
