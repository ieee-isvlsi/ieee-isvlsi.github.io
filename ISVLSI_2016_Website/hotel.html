ISVLSI’16 – Venue, Travel, and Hotel Information

 

Venue:

Conference will be held at University of Pittsburgh, the University Club (https://uc.pitt.edu/):

Address: 123 University Place, Pittsburgh, PA 15260

 

Travel:

The nearest airport is the Pittsburgh International Airport (code: PIT), which is around 21 miles away from the conference venue. It takes approximately 20-25 minutes by taxi and 45 minutes via public transportation (Bus 28X by PortAuthority) to reach from the airport to the University Club.

For maps and directions:

http://uc.pitt.edu/wedding-banquets-conferences/location/#go_here
