The Conference organizers have planned several exciting social events for the ISVLSI'16 attendees! Please read the details below:

 

Welcome Reception (Sponsored by IEEE CEDA PA Chapter)

July 10, 2016 (Sunday): 5:00pm ~ 7:00pm

Rooftop Terrence, the University Club (https://uc.pitt.edu/)

Address: 123 University Place, Pittsburgh, PA 15260

 

Conference Banquet Dinner

July 11, 2016 (Monday): 6:15pm ~ 9:00pm

Grand Concourse (http://www.grandconcourserestaurant.com/)

Seated dinner. Buses will pick up the attendees at the University Club and drop off at the same place.

 

Poster Session and Light Snack

July 12, 2016 (Tuesday): 5:50pm ~ 6:50pm

Light snack and drink will be provided.

 

The Molly’s Trolley City Tour

July 12, 2016 (Tuesday): 7:00pm ~ 9:00pm

http://www.mollystrolleyspittsburgh.com/

Meeting Location: The lobby of the University Club (https://uc.pitt.edu/)

Closing Remarks and Award Ceremony (Ballroom B)

July 13, 2016 (Wednesday): 3:00pm ~ 3:30pm

Ballroom B of University Club

Best Paper Award, Students Travel Grants Award, and Best Poster Awards will announced during closing ceremony. Authors are recommended to attend.

 

Lunches and Coffee Breaks

Lunch will be provided for all attendees daily. Additionally, coffee breaks will take place in the late morning and late afternoon.
