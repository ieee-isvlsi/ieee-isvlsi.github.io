About Author Registration:
  • Each accepted paper needs to have at least one regular registration at $250 or $300. Otherwise, the paper will be removed from the conference program.
  • For Regular Papers: Paper length is limited to 6 pages and up to 2 extra pages (for a maximum length of 8 pages)
  • For Invited / Special Issue Papers: paper length is limited to 6 pages and up to 4 extra pages (for a maximum length of 10 pages)
  • The page fee is $200 per extra page. The extra page charge needs to be paid at the time of registration.
