Venue Information

 

ABOUT THE VENUE

The Conference will be held at Aliathon Resort in Pafos, Cyprus located in the heart of the Pafos tourist area, easily accessible to the harbour and town.

The Aliathon Resort is an oasis of beauty and tranquillity oriented towards families and couples interested in high quality holidays. It comprises of two themed villages, the Aliathon Aegean and the Aliathon Ionian Village, set in an estate of 14 hectares, as well as the Aliathon Plaza collection of restaurants and bars.

Aliathon is also an ideal venue for corporate meetings, incentives, conferences and exhibitions as it can accommodate the needs of all conference delegates who wish to combine business and pleasure. Magnificent surroundings and stylish furnishings ensure that delegates are able to work in comfort.

In particular, the conference will take place at the Conferences & Multi-function Center. See below for details.

Address: Theas Afroditis Avenue, Yeroskipou 8201, Pafos, Cyprus

Directions: Aliathon Resort

 

 

Coming from the airport:

If you are arriving at Pafos International Airport, the most convenient option is to take public bus route #612, which will take you directly at Aliathon Resort (bus stop "Poseidonos 6"). This bus route has a finishing point the Kato Paphos Central Station, which is situated near the city centre.

If you are arriving at Larnaca International Airport, Kapnos Airport Shuttle Bus transfers you from Larnaca Airport to Pafos Airport with only €15 per adult, €5 per child and €10 per student, one way (including VAT and luggage). From there, you can use the public bus mentioned above (#612) to transfer you at the conference venue. For further information please visit the official website of Cyprus By Bus and the Kapnos Airport Shuttle.

Public Taxis are always available at both airports. The price for a taxi from Larnaca Airport to Aliathon Resort is approximately €100 and from Paphos Airport is approximately €30. Click here for the guide to the Taxi Services in Cyprus, as issued by the Ministry of Communications and Works.

The route from Larnaca Airport to Aliathon takes about 1 hour and 30 minutes.

The route from Paphos Airport to Aliathon takes about 25 minutes.
